About Me

Hi, I'm Devy, a data scientist with a background in software development. With two years of experience as a software developer in Indonesia, I gained valuable skills before embarking on a working holiday in Australia. Throughout my journey, I've had the opportunity to work in diverse industries and assume various roles, contributing to my personal and professional growth.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from BINUS University in Indonesia, providing me with a strong foundation in computer science principles. Additionally, I pursued further education and obtained a Graduate Certificate in Artificial Intelligence Analysis, Design & Implementation from Durham College in Ontario, Canada.
This program equipped me with advanced knowledge in data preparation, visualization, and the ability to elucidate how data can support companies in making well-informed decisions.

One of my latest project involved with the development of a web application for an AI-based attendance system. By leveraging computer vision and facial recognition technologies, we created an efficient and accurate solution for attendance management. This experience allowed me to apply my skills in building models and designing AI-based web applications, empowering organizations with intelligent tools beyond traditional applications.
I also have experience in the following technologies:
