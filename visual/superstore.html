Superstore Data Analysis

In this comprehensive data analysis project, we will dig into Sample Superstore data from Tableau. Throughout this project, we will examine various aspects of Superstore operations. From sales performance and customer behaviour to inventory management and operational efficiency. We will explore every facet to extract meaningful patterns and trends.

Also, identify top-performing products, customer segments and geographic regions, enabling us to optimize pricing strategies, identify untapped markets, and amplify revenue growth. By analyzing customer behaviour, we will understand their preferences, purchasing patterns, and satisfaction levels. This will be allowing us to personalize marketing efforts, enhance customer loyalty, and increase customer lifetime value.

  • Dataset

What is our customer acquisition trend?

In 2017, we were consistently acquiring new customers per week. However, it looks like it has dropped starting from 2018. We need to approach a different business strategy to break the current pattern. We also can create a campaign that attract a new customers.
For example: introduction voucher for new customer or free shipping for new customer with certain purchase.

How many customers do we have per yearly cohort?

In 2017 there are 595 customers that made their first transaction. Meanwhile, only 517 of them made another transaction in 2020. In spite of the fact that the number does not decline drastically, we still want to maintain to have less of it.

How often do the customers purchase from our store?

It seems that most of our customer at least made more than 1 transaction. The majority of them made 5 repeat order. When we break it down by year, in 2020 the number of customers that purchase more than 5 is increased. We can create a point system for the current customers that can be converted as vouchers for future use, to encourage the customer to keep making purchases.
