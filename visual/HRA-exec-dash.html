Human Resources Executive Dashboard

In today's competitive job market, businesses are placing higher value on their workforce management initiatives. It is essential to ensure that employees are content and satisfied in their work environment, as this can have a considerable impact on productivity and profitability.
This interactive HR Analytics dashboard is created using dummy data published by IBM's team. The dashboard is divided into 4 categories: costs, satisfaction, diversification, and attrition.
